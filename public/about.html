Prophetess Shelsy Royal Tinker

Prophetess Shelsy Royal is a chosen and set-apart servant of God, faithfully walking in her prophetic calling for over six years across Hyderabad, Kurnool, and Chennai. Praise God so for such a powerful and anointed Daughter of God
